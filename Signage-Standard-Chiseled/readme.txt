Signage Standard
a Truetype font created by Ryland Sanders

Signage Standard, Signage Standard Chiseled, Signage Modern, and Signage Geometric
are based on the popular styles of changeable lettering for roadside signage.

TrueType and OpenType versions of the font are included in the distribution
package.

These fonts are not for sale. They may be distributed freely and included in font
collections as long as no charge is made for them.
